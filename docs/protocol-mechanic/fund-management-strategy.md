# Fund Management Strategy

Your funds don't just sit idle in SuperCluster—they're actively managed to optimize returns while maintaining safety and liquidity. Think of it as having a professional portfolio manager working 24/7 for your stablecoins.

## Active vs. Passive Management

**Without active management:**

- Stuck with one platform's rates
- All funds at risk if that platform has issues
- Can't capture better opportunities elsewhere

**With SuperCluster's active management:**

- Funds automatically move to best opportunities
- Risk spread across multiple platforms
- Consistent optimization without your effort

It's like having a system that automatically moves your money between the best savings accounts, always getting you the highest safe returns.

## How the Strategy Works

The system continuously balances three priorities:

```
High Yield + Strong Safety + Ready Liquidity = Optimal Allocation
```

**It does this by:**

- Monitoring rates across lending platforms in real-time
- Evaluating safety and liquidity of each platform
- Rebalancing when better opportunities appear
- Maintaining reserves for quick withdrawals

**Example:** If Platform A offers 5% but Platform B increases to 6%, the system reallocates funds to capture that higher yield—automatically.

**What you experience:** Your balance grows faster. Everything else happens behind the scenes.

## Three Strategy Approaches

SuperCluster adapts its approach based on market conditions:

| Strategy            | Focus                        | When Used                   |
| ------------------- | ---------------------------- | --------------------------- |
| **Conservative**    | Safety first, stable returns | Market uncertainty          |
| **Balanced**        | Optimal risk-return mix      | Normal conditions (default) |
| **Yield-Optimized** | Maximum safe returns         | Strong market opportunities |

Strategy selection is automatic—the system chooses based on current conditions.

## What You Don't Have to Worry About

**Manual monitoring** — System tracks rates 24/7, you don't need to check
