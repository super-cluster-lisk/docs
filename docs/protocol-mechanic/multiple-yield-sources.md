# Multiple Yield Sources

SuperCluster doesn't rely on just one lending platform—your funds are distributed across multiple established platforms to optimize returns while reducing risk. This is like spreading your savings across different banks instead of putting everything in one place.

## Don't Put All Eggs in One Basket

Imagine choosing where to save money:

**Option A:** Everything in one bank at 5% interest  
**Option B:** Split across two banks offering 4.8% and 5.2%

Most people choose Option B because:

- If one bank has problems, you don't lose everything
- You get the best rates from each
- You're not dependent on a single institution

SuperCluster applies this same principle to DeFi lending.

## Where Your Yield Comes From

Your USDC earns interest from established lending platforms where borrowers pay to use stablecoins:

**Morpho** — Efficient lending protocol with strong yields (typically 4-6% APY)  
**Aave** — Largest DeFi lending platform with maximum security (typically 3-5% APY)

```
Your Deposit → SuperCluster → Split between Morpho & Aave → Borrowers pay interest → You earn yield
```

**What you do:** Deposit once  
**What you get:** Yield from multiple sources, consolidated in one balance

## Why Multiple Sources Matter

### Better Returns

Platforms offer different rates at different times. SuperCluster allocates more to higher-yielding platforms automatically.

**Example:** If Aave offers 5% and Morpho offers 6%, more funds go to Morpho. When rates change, the system rebalances to maintain optimal returns.

### Lower Risk

Spreading funds across platforms means if one has issues, only a portion of your deposit is affected—not everything.

**Think of it like:** Having $5,000 in two banks instead of $10,000 in one. If one fails, you've still protected half your funds.

### Better Liquidity

Large withdrawals can be sourced from multiple platforms simultaneously, ensuring you get your funds quickly without delays.

## How It Works Behind the Scenes

SuperCluster connects to each lending platform through specialized pathways that handle deposits, withdrawals, yield collection, and monitoring. Each platform works differently, but the system handles all the complexity—you just interact with SuperCluster.

**Your experience:**

- One deposit → distributed automatically
- One balance → grows from multiple sources
- One withdrawal → consolidated from all platforms

## Platform Selection Standards

Not every lending platform makes the cut. Only platforms that meet strict criteria are connected:

**Security:** Proven track record, professional audits, strong team  
**Liquidity:** Deep reserves, fast withdrawals, reliable availability  
**Economics:** Sustainable yields, competitive rates, transparent fees

Only established, battle-tested platforms are used—no experimental or high-risk options.

## Easy to Expand, Easy to Remove

New high-quality platforms can be added as they emerge, giving you access to more opportunities. If a platform becomes problematic, it can be removed without disrupting your earnings from other sources.

**You benefit from:** Growing opportunities  
**You're protected from:** Stuck exposure to declining platforms

## Safety Note

While diversification offers significant benefits, understand the risks:

**You're still exposed to each platform's risks.** If a connected platform experiences losses, SuperCluster users share that loss proportionally.

**How we mitigate this:**

- Only connect to proven, audited platforms
- Limit allocation to any single platform
- Monitor continuously for issues
- Maintain liquidity reserves

Diversification reduces impact but doesn't eliminate risk entirely.
